Who are we?

Greetings from the minds behind the movement! We are Johnny Jones and Will Carlson, the co-founders of Driver’s Club. Born from a shared vision of a more connected and compassionate Europe, we've been steering the wheel towards positive change through our unique rallies.

Our journey began with a mutual belief in the power of collective action. As seasoned travelers and advocates, we recognized the potential of rallying communities across Europe to amplify our impact. Driver’s Club emerged as our vessel to turn this vision into a reality.
